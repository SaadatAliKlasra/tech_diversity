PARTNERS

We offer various sponsorship packages tailored to meet your organization's goals and demonstrate your commitment to diversity and inclusion in the tech industry. By partnering with us, you will directly support our initiatives to:

  • Provide scholarships and financial aid to underrepresented students pursuing degrees in technology fields
  • Organize workshops, webinars, and mentorship programs designed to develop skills and provide networking opportunities for underrepresented individuals in tech.
  • Create and maintain an online platform that offers resources, guidance, and support for diverse tech professionals at all stages of their careers.
  • Partner with schools and educational institutions to promote computer science and technology education in underserved communities.

Sponsorship Benefits

By sponsoring our organization, you will enjoy the following benefits:

  • Brand Visibility: Showcase your organization's commitment to diversity and inclusion by being featured on our website, social media platforms, and event materials.
  • Networking Opportunities: Connect with a diverse pool of talent, industry experts, and other partner organizations at our events and workshops.
  • Talent Recruitment: Gain access to a broad network of diverse and talented professionals interested in working for organizations that value diversity and inclusion.
  • Community Impact: Contribute to the growth and success of underrepresented individuals in the tech industry, driving positive change in the sector.

How to Become a Sponsor

If you are interested in partnering with us and supporting our mission, please follow these steps:

  • Review our sponsorship packages and select the one that best aligns with your organization's goals.
  • Complete our online sponsorship application form, providing your organization's details and the sponsorship package you wish to select.
  • Our team will review your application and contact you to discuss the next steps in the partnership process.
